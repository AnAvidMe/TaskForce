Objective

The main objective of Task Force is to make the life of students at NIT Andhra Pradesh easier and provide special opportunities for the students to take part in administrative activities

It is a student initiative with the aim to aid and support the NIT Andhra Pradesh students. It is not an alternate body to the Studentsâ€™ Council, but rather a supporting body which aims to bridge the gap between the administration and the students related to the various events organised on and off the campus. It is an Autonomous student body, directly under the Director of the Institute.

TaskForce NIT Andhra Pradesh will comprise of eight modules, which will seek to address the various issues faced by students at college and also help them stay well-informed regarding the happenings on campus.

The main objective lies in making a student life here easier right from his/her admission in first year to convocation in final year and alumni support after graduation. TaskForce is the official Executive body & Reformation body. It can represent public issues to the admin and request for support.
